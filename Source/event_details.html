Event Details

The CCIM Business Excellence Awards is a premier annual event put together by the CCIM Utah Chapter. This "red-carpet" style black-tie optional awards honors and showcases top performers, superior service, innovative ideas and design and will bring together more than 500 top business leaders and professionals from the commercial real estate sector. The 2014 awards event will be held at Salt Lake City Marriott City Center (75 South West Temple) on February 22, 2014.

  • 5:30 PM - 7:00 PM Cocktails & Networking
  • 7:00 PM - 7:45 PM Dinner
  • 7:45 PM - 9:00 PM Program
