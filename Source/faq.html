Frequently Asked Questions

What are the CCIM Business Excellence Awards?

The CCIM Business Excellence Awards is a premier annual event put together by the Utah CCIM Chapter. This "red-carpet" style black tie optional event will recognize and honor the accomplishments of Utah commercial real estate practitioners who exemplify the highest degree of business excellence.

Who can be nominated and how do they get nominated?

Anyone can be nominated. You can nominate yourself, colleagues, partners, superiors or friends for the great example they set in the commercial real estate sector.

Do I need to be a CCIM or Utah Chapter Member to receive the award?

No. Although membership is always welcome. For more information on becoming a member of the Utah Chapter, contact admin@utahccimchapter.com

What is the difference between nominations and applications?

Colleagues, partners or friends will put your name in to "nominate" you. Once nominated, you will be asked to fill out the "application" with details we can distribute to our distinguished selection committee and judging panel.

How does the judging process work/Who are the judges?

Once your application has been received, the board of directors for the Utah CCIM Chapter will review the applications of each candidate and a selection will be made of the top 3 candidates in each award category for placement on a ballot to be distributed to distinguished Utah business, civic, government, and industry leaders for a final review and vote.

What are the award categories?

  • Top Broker of the Year (New!)
  • Rookie of the Year (New!)
  • Top Real Estate Transaction of the Year (New!)
  • Industrial Broker of the Year
  • Investment Broker of the Year
  • Retail Broker of the Year
  • Office Broker of the Year
  • Architectural Firm of the Year
  • Contractor of the Year
  • Development/Redevelopment of the Year
  • Property Manager of the Year
  • Real Estate Attorney of the Year
  • Real Estate Banker of the Year(NEW Split Award Categories)
      • Commercial Real Estate (loans over $50 million)
      Mid Market Real Estate (loans under $50 million)

What do recipients receive?

  • Custom Designed, Engraved Desktop Award
  • Photo and bio in our Utah Business Excellence Magazine
    to be distributed after event
  • Title and Award along with its prestige and honor

When will the event be held?

February 22, 2014 at the Salt Lake City Marriott City Center (75 South West Temple).
