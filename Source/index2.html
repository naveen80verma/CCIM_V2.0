The CCIM Business Excellence Awards (CCIM BEA) recognizes and honors the accomplishments of Utah commercial real estate practitioners who exemplify the highest degree of business excellence. CCIM BEA showcases top performers, superior service, innovative ideas and designs, and continue to foster and promote business excellence in Utah commercial real estate.
