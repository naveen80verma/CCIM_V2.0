Judging Criteria
  • Vision of the project and impact on community (25 points) - Please judge the project on its overall impact on the community (e.g. how does it impact and foster community growth, vitality, longevity, and stability)
  • Innovative Design (25 points) - Judge the project on its design characteristics (e.g. creativity, sustainability, construction quality, etc.)
  • Highest and Best use for the site (25 points) - Judge the project on its use(s). Score it based on your opinion of whether this project is the right use(s) for this site and whether it is a good fir for the location and neighborhood. Though not a determinant, economic feasibility is also a factor in this criteria and the applicant should discuss it. However, there is no expectation of disclosing confidential information.
  • Reputation (20 points) - Judge the developer on its reputation based on the submittal and testimonial(s).
  • Quality of application (5 points)


In a single pdf file of four pages or less, please provide information, pictures, project details, and narrative about a portfolio of no more than three properties.
